Our Projects

Here are some of the projects that are coming soon...

Project Description
Six Flags (Qiddya) A massive theme park near Riyadh and located in Dhurma, designed with six themed lands, thrill rides, family attractions, and the record-breaking Falcon’s Flight roller coaster. It’s set to be one of the world’s most exciting amusement destinations.
Aquarabia A world-class water park in Qiddiya, featuring wave pools, lazy rivers, and Arabian-inspired designs. It will be one of the largest in the region, offering both high-speed slides and relaxing water experiences for families.
